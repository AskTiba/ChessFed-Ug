# 🔮 Future Features & Community Roadmap

This document serves as the **Idea Forge** for ChessFed Uganda. We welcome the community and contributors to pick up any of these "epic" tasks or suggest new ones. Our goal is to move beyond a simple leaderboard and build a living, breathing ecosystem for Ugandan chess.

---

## 🏅 1. Player Achievements & Prestige
*Moving from ELO numbers to a legacy-based profile.*

*   **Achievement Badges:** Render visual trophies/medals on profiles for:
    *   Tournament Podiums (1st, 2nd, 3rd).
    *   Title Gains (National Master, Candidate Master, etc.).
    *   National Representation (Representing the "Cranes" at Olympiads).
*   **Performance Timeline:** A "Career Journey" chart showing ELO peaks and valleys over the years.
*   **Virtual Trophy Room:** A dedicated section on the profile to display won trophies in high-fidelity 3D/SVG models.

## 🤝 2. Community & "The Federation Lifestyle"
*Defining what it means to be a member beyond the board.*

*   **The "Cage Match" Arena:** A system to challenge other members to high-stakes "private" matches with a live "Banter & Hype" chat section.
*   **Federation SACCO Dashboard:** Integrated financial tools for the Chess SACCO (Savings and Credit Co-operatives), allowing members to track savings, loans, and dividends.
*   **The "Off-Board" Hub:** Organizing and tracking federation-wide football matches, get-togethers, and charity fundraising events.
*   **Banter Box:** A community-wide social feed where members can share "brilliancies," blunders, and healthy rivalry talk.

## 🏆 3. Advanced League & Club Dynamics
*Deepening the connection between players and their clubs.*

*   **Transfer Market:** A live feed of latest club transfers, including "Historical Club History" on player profiles (which clubs they played for since 2010).
*   **Club Roster Deep-Dives:** Ability to click into any club and see a full list of players with mini-stat previews.
*   **Interactive League Table:** Filter league standings by Region, Season, or Division.

## 📊 4. Game Analytics & History
*The technical core of a chess platform.*

*   **PGN Archive:** Every game played in an official federation event should be searchable, viewable, and filterable.
*   **Head-to-Head Stats:** Compare two players to see their historical win/loss record against each other.
*   **Tournament Performance Filters:** Sort game history by opening, result, or tournament prestige.

---

## 💡 AI-Suggested Features (The "Extra Mile")
*Ideas proposed by your Gemini Peer Programmer to make the hub world-class.*

*   **Digital Federation ID:** A system to generate a secure, downloadable, and printable PDF Federation Membership Card with a QR code for tournament check-ins.
*   **Mentorship Bridge:** A "Find a Coach" feature where senior titled players can list themselves as mentors for upcoming youth talents.
*   **AI-Driven Game Summaries:** Automatically generate a written "Match Report" summary for top-board league matches using LLMs.
*   **Daily Chess UG Blog:** A CMS section where federation journalists and players can submit tournament reports, news, and "Ugandan Opening" theory.
*   **Arbitration Portal:** A secure way for players to submit "Correction Requests" or official appeals to the Technical Committee.
